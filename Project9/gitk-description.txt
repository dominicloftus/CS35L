The top window shows the list of commits that has been made to the master
branch since it was created with a log description on the left, name of
contributer in the middle and modification date on the right

The bottom left shows the diff file used in the commit and also has the option
of viewing the old and new versions.

The bottom right shows the files that were changed in the commit and has
the option to view the entire tree of files contained in the branch.
